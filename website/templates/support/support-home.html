Seeking Help?
As a site for young people, we're also committed to helping and supplying as much resources as possible for anyone to reflect and possibly seek help from professionals, we understand that not all wounds are physical nor the same, clicking on the sidebar will pop up a menu redirecting you to possible subjects you may find interest within.

Remember your not alone and there are multiple people beside you with the same experiences as you and that it doesn't invalidate your experiences anymore then theirs.
See Someone Needing Help?
If you see someone going through a rough patch within life go and talk to them, be that reaching hand to help them out, you'd be surprised how much a talk can help out one's situation.
Remember To Understand Boundaries!
Always extend a helping hand but remember to not over extend to where others are not comfortable with, some topics can be hard to talk about so when helping others remember to take your time with it.
Need Help Yourself?
If you need help you can find the appropriate page and it'll point you to the appropriate resources and contacts for you to get further help.
Just A Disclaimer
Even though we're not experts within the topics we may cover we have gone to the greatest lengths to make sure all information is correct, well cited, and that all contacts shown are correctly linked and are well accredited for, if you need even further help please seek professional help as a website with bullet points isn't enough to diagnose you.
