The Zillo website was created specifically for young audiances living within the Wirral peninsula to engauge within the community and spread, their ideas, experiences, and thoughts on the happenings of the community.

Here we offer a hub of articles, events and voluntering work to do, all to get younger folk out and about, especially with our Young Reporters Initiative that enables young folk to publish their own opinions, view points and interests to a larger audiance, so please support us and them!

We can also thank our upbringing and website to the kind folks over at the Wirral Metropolitan College, starting in April 2020 we've always tried working as close as possible with web development students to transform Zillo into a website made by the youth for the youth, we went from consept to beta sites all the way to a fully fleged website, all completed as a part of their final exams to get them ready for a real one-to-one web development discussion.

As our partnership with the folks at Wirral Metropolitan College grow and progress you can surely expect more from us within the near future providing more oppertunities for students across the college.
- Us At Zillo
